biography: Ruth Kundsin was a microbiologist and Associate Professor of Microbiology and Molecular Genetics at Harvard Medical School. Kundsin's scientific contributions to the field of microbiology, specifically her research on airborne pathogens, led to important changes in hospitals and operating rooms that are still followed to this day. She published over 150 scholarly articles and five books. Kundsin was also a Fellow in the American Academy of Microbiology as well as the New York Academy of Sciences.
