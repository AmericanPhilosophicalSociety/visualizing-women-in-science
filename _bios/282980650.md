biography: Laura Florence was a British entomologist. Born in Scotland, Florence was educated at the University of Aberdeen. In 1914, she moved to the United States to continue her education, earning an MA from Stanford in 1916 and a PhD from Cornell University in 1920. Her dissertation focused on pig lice. She later became a professor of bacteriology at New York Medical College.
