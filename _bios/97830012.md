biography: Caroline Bamberger Frank Fuld, along with her brother Louis Bamberger, donated and financially endowed funds for what would become the Institute of Advanced Study in Princeton. The Bamberger family were prominent department store owners in Baltimore. After speaking with Abraham Flexner in 1922, Fuld and her brother contributed over 23 million to begin and endow the Institute. Fuld was a supporter of Florence Sabin and wrote that she felt she had "discovered" her talent.
