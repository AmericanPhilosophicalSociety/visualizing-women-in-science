biography: Elaine P. Ralli was an Associate Professor of Medicine at the New York University School of Medicine. She was an authority on diseases of metabolism and nutrition, particularly diabetes, and undertook a major project to greatly increase the capacity of the metabolism clinics at the Bellevue Hospital in the 1950s. She received the Elizabeth Blackwell Citation for her outstanding achievements as a woman in medicine.
