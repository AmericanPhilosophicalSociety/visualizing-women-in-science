biography: Mary E. Pinney was a biologist and zoologist. She was educated in biology at the University of Kansas, receiving her A.B. in 1908 and her A.M. in 1910. In 1912, she held the Naples Table and conducted research at the Marine Zoological Station in Naples. She received her PhD in biology in 1917 from Bryn Mawr College. From 1924 to 1929, she was a professor of zoology at Milwaukee-Downer College.
