biography: Mary C. Pangborn was an American biochemist and science fiction writer. She received her PhD from Yale in 1931. She worked at the Department of Health in Albany and was the senior author for the World Health Organization Report on Cardiolipin Anigens in 1951. In 1979, she published her first work of fiction, and would go on to publish several additional short stories before her death.
