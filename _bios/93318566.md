biography: Maria Erecińska is a biochemical and medical researcher. Born in Poland, Erecińska spent much of her career at the University of Pennsylvania as a professor of pharmacology in the School of Medicine. Her main area of research is metabolism, with particular emphasis on metabolism in the brain. Her studies on metabolism in the brain have implications for the study of degenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's.
