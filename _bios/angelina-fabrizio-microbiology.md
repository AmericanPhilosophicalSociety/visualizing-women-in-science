biography: Angelina Fabrizio was an American microbiologist and cancer pathologist. Fabrizio completed graduate studies at the University of Pennsylvania under the supervision of Florence Seibert and earned her PhD in 1952 with a dissertation on tuberculin in tissue cultures. Fabrizio held research positions at numerous hospitals in the Philadelphia area, eventually becoming an Associate Professor of Microbiology at Thomas Jefferson University. Her later research focused on cancer pathology.
