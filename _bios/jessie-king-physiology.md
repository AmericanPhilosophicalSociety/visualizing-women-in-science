biography: Jessie Luella King was an American physiologist. King was one of the first women to receive a doctorate in physiology in the United States, graduating from Cornell Medical College in 1911. King taught physiology at Goucher College, where she was colleagues with Lilian Welsh. While a student at Goucher College, Florence Seibert took courses from King. King's research concerned the physiology of menstruation and reproduction.
