biography: Caroline Burling Thompson was an American entomologist. Thompson completed her education at the University of Pennsylvania, earning her BS in biology in 1898 and her PhD in 1901. Afterwards, she took a position at Wellesley College, where she taught zoology. Her researched focused on the brains of ants and termites as well as the social organization of bees and termites.She held the Naples Table at the Marine Biological Station in Naples in 1913.
