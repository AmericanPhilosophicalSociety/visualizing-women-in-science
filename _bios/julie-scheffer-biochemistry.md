biography: Julie Scheffler is an American biochemist. Her research concerns the structure of biomolecules. Scheffler received her PhD in 1980 from West Virginia University. Afterwards, Scheffler was a post-doctoral research assistant in Mildred Cohn's lab. She received a position at Iowa State University, but transitioned to industry in the late 1980s. She has had a successful career in pharmaceuticals, working for Bristol-Myers Squibb and Johnson & Johnson.
