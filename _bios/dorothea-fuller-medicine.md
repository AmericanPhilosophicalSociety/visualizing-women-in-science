description: Dorothea Schallert Fuller, has a LOR written by Kenneth C. Smithburn to go to study tropical medicine in Puerto Rico. She published an article "A Note on the Staining of Tubercle Bacilli in Sections" for the Journal of Laboratory and Clinical Medicine, Sabin writes to Dr. Gasser, head of Rockefeller Institute in 1937 to ask if it is ok that Mrs Fuller publish her article. "Mrs Fuller who does all of our section cutting, as you know, has worked out a method for staining tubercle bacilli which we have found of such value that we want her to publish it... She has a Master's degree and has done part of the work for a Doctor's degree." June 8, 1937, Herbert S. Gasser 1936-39, Box 9.
