biography: Mary Florence Denton was an American educator who spent most of her career at Doshishia University in Kyoto. While in Japan, Denton fostered research exchanges between the United States and East Asia. It was in this capacity that she corresponded with Florence Sabin. Sabin wrote to Denton to introduce her to Louise Pearce who had plans to work at Peiping Union Medical College in China.
